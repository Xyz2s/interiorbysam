About Us

At Interior By Sam, we believe every space has a story. Our mission is to bring your vision to life with creativity, elegance, and functionality. With over a decade of experience, our passionate team of designers and craftsmen work closely with clients to create bespoke interiors that reflect their personality and lifestyle. We specialize in both residential and commercial projects, offering a full spectrum of services from concept development and space planning to final installation. Our philosophy is rooted in attention to detail, sustainability, and timeless design. Whether you are looking to refresh a single room or transform an entire property, we are dedicated to delivering results that exceed expectations. Let us help you create a space that is beautiful, functional, and uniquely yours.

Our Services

Residential Design

We create personalized home interiors that blend comfort, style, and functionality. Our residential design services include space planning, color consultation, furniture selection, lighting design, and custom decor solutions. We work with you to craft inviting living spaces, functional kitchens, and serene bedrooms tailored to your lifestyle and preferences.

Commercial Design

We design modern, efficient workspaces that inspire productivity and reflect your brand. Our commercial design services cover offices, retail spaces, hospitality, and more. We focus on optimizing layouts, enhancing workflow, and creating environments that leave a lasting impression on clients and employees alike.

Renovation & Remodeling

Transform your space with our expert renovation and remodeling solutions. From minor updates to complete makeovers, we manage every aspect of the renovation process, including project management, material selection, and quality control. Our goal is to deliver beautiful, functional spaces on time and within budget.
